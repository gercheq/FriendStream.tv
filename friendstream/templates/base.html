Friendstream.tv broadcasts videos shared by your friends

Friendstream.tv is an application that broadcasts the videos shared by your friends. You can connect your social network accounts like Facebook and Twitter to friendstream.tv and watch non-stop videos shared by your friends on those services. You can also comment on and like videos, and reach them anytime through a personalized archive search.

Friendstream.tv is reachable through your computer, mobile device and television, and provides a simplified online viewing experience for people who get a ton of video recommendations throughout the day. When you just want to relax and be entertained, wouldn’t you rather have relevant content delivered to you instead of having to search for it? Friendstream.tv is for everyone who is tired of channel-surfing and never finding the show that they want. In addition to bringing all your friends’ video links into one place, it can also be a personalized channel on your TV of what your friends are watching.

Friendstream.tv will work on your Internet friendly television as an application. For non-internet enabled televisions additional hardware will provide a link to friendstream.tv. The application can be controlled remotely with laptops, tablets and smart phones.

Being launched in the Silicon Valley StartUp Bus competition, friendstream.tv is pioneering a new generation of television at SXSW 2011. The company is currently seed funded and raising its first round.
